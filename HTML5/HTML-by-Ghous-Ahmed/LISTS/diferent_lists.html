Scrambled Eggs

Egges are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbs Butter
  • 2 tbs Cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a fry over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add these Two Ingrediants:
    • Cream
    • Eggs
  4. Using the spatcula folds the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from th heat

Terms and Their Meanings

Medium Heat
medium heat is typically 350 to 375 degrees Fahrenheit or 180 to 190 degrees Celsius.
Spatcula
A spatula is a broad, flat, flexible blade used to mix, spread and lift material including foods, drugs, plaster and paints.
Moist
something slightly or moderately wet, tearful, or characterized by high humidity.
