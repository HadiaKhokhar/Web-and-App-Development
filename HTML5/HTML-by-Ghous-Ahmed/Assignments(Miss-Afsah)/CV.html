The Significance of Structural and Semantic Markup:

Structural Markup:

Structural markup, achieved through tags like "head", "body", "div", "header", and "footer", provides the skeleton for a webpage, defining its layout and sections.

Semantic Markup:

Semantic markup adds meaning to content, making it more accessible and comprehensible for both developers and search engines. Basically semantic markups provide additional information about the content that is between that tags. Some example of Semantic Tags include "emphasis", "italic", "bold", "Strong", "br", "hr", "superscript", "subscrit", "blockquote", "quote" etc.
